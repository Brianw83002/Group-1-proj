About Us

Authors:
Leiss Amini, Eduardo Melendez, Chris Saran, Linh Tran, Brian Williams

For travelers seeking a place to stay during vacations or events, Airhome offers a smart solution for finding the perfect home. Users can filter listings by price--from lowest to highest--making it easy to discover recommended properties that suit their needs. Unlike other rental platforms that charge excessive fees, AirHome provides affordable rates, flexible options, and a user-friendly interface.
